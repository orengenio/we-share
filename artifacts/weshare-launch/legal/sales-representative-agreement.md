## SALES REPRESENTATIVE AGREEMENT

This Sales Representative Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and effective upon the
Sales Representative's electronic acceptance, recorded with version, date, and time
**[ADAPTED: electronic acceptance replaces the paper date line]**,

**BETWEEN:** **OrenGen Worldwide LLC** (the "Company"), a limited liability company
organized and existing under the laws of the State of **Texas**, with its head office
located at: **Mansfield, Texas** *(registered address on file)*,

**AND:** the **Sales Representative** — the individual identified by the WeShare
account through which this Agreement is accepted, an independent contractor with the
contact address provided at registration. **[ADAPTED: identity binds to the
authenticated account rather than a typed name block]**

### Sales Representative agrees to:

1. Represent and sell the Company's website services in the geographic area known as
   the **United States (non-exclusive; no exclusive territory is granted)**. **[ADAPTED]**
2. Accurately represent and state Company policies to all potential and present
   customers, including fixed pricing of **$997 setup + $247/month** collected only
   through the Company's official checkout, and to make no claims beyond the
   Company's approved claims. **[ADAPTED]**
3. Promptly log all leads and orders in the Company's WeShare platform — same-day
   logging; the platform record is the system of record for commissions. **[ADAPTED
   from "promptly mail in all leads and orders"]**
4. Inform the Company of all problems concerning Company customers within the sales
   territory, via the platform or partners@orengen.io.
5. Inform the Company if the Sales Representative is representing, or plans to
   represent, any other business firm. In no event shall the Sales Representative
   represent a competitive company or product line either within or outside the
   designated sales area.
6. Maintain reasonable contact with the Company, including making first contact with
   each assigned lead within **four (4) hours** of assignment per the Partner
   Handbook. **[ADAPTED from "telephone with reasonable frequency"]**
7. Provide the Company **fourteen (14) days'** notice should the Representative
   intend to terminate this Agreement.
8. Cease use of, and where applicable return, all materials and access provided by
   the Company — including scripts, the Partner Handbook, CRM access, marketing
   materials, and any Company-assigned phone number — if either party terminates
   this Agreement. **[ADAPTED for digital materials]**

### The Company agrees to:

1. Pay the following commissions to the Sales Representative:
   - **25% of the setup fee** on each completed sale closed by the Representative, and
   - **25% of the monthly maintenance fee** for each such client, **for the life of
     the client account**, subject to paragraphs 3 and 4 below. Commissions become
     payout-eligible **fifteen (15) days** after the underlying payment clears
     (NET-15 maturity) and are paid via Stripe Connect on the Company's scheduled
     payout runs. **[ADAPTED: fills the % placeholders; prepaid/credit distinction
     replaced by the platform's single official-checkout flow]**
2. To negotiate in advance of sale the commission treatment of any order on which
   the Company allows a discount or other trade concession. (Company pricing is
   currently fixed; no discounts are offered.)
3. Commissions on refunds to customers in which a commission has already been paid
   to the Representative shall be deducted from future commissions to be paid to the
   Representative by the Company — including negative adjustments netted against
   subsequent payouts. Refunds within thirty (30) days of payment void the
   associated commission. **[ADAPTED: clawback window + netting mechanics]**
4. Except by special arrangement, the following shall not be commissioned: sales not
   completed through the Company's official checkout; purchases made by the
   Representative on their own behalf; refunded sales; and amounts attributable to
   taxes or payment-processing adjustments. **[ADAPTED: fills the [List]]**
5. To provide the Sales Representative with reasonable sales materials in digital
   form — the Partner Handbook, approved scripts, CRM access, the marketing
   materials library, and, upon certification, a Company-assigned business phone
   number. **[ADAPTED from cards/brochures/samples]**
6. To set minimum activity standards after consultation with the Sales
   Representative, as published in the Partner Handbook (including the four-hour
   first-touch standard and same-day logging). Lead assignments begin upon
   certification. **[ADAPTED from "minimum monthly quotas"]**
7. To grant the Representative **fourteen (14) days'** notice should the Company
   wish to terminate this Agreement — except that the Company may terminate
   immediately for cause, meaning fraud, client circumvention, or material breach of
   this Agreement, the Program Terms of Service, or the Partner Handbook
   (§§9–12). **[ADAPTED: defines "cause" — closes risk-map finding V1]**
8. To pay commissions to the Representative on sales from existing clients after
   this Agreement is terminated as follows: where the Representative exits in good
   standing, residual commissions under paragraph 1 continue **for the life of each
   client account closed by the Representative**; where the Company terminates for
   cause, unpaid and future commissions are forfeited. **[ADAPTED: the template's
   fixed post-termination months replaced by the program's published
   lifetime-residual promise — flag for counsel]**

### Additional provisions **[ADDED — from the platform's operating terms]**

9. **Independent contractor.** The Representative is an independent contractor, not
   an employee, and is solely responsible for their own taxes. Taxpayer information
   (W-9/TIN) is collected and certified through Stripe Connect, and tax forms
   (e.g., Form 1099) are issued through Stripe where thresholds are met.
10. **Incorporation by reference.** The WeShare Program Terms of Service (including
    its Texas governing-law and Tarrant County arbitration provisions, limitation of
    liability, and indemnification) and the Partner Handbook are incorporated into
    and form part of this Agreement. In case of conflict, this Agreement controls as
    to commission terms. *(Closes risk-map findings G1–G3.)*
11. **Confidentiality and non-circumvention.** Client relationships, lead data,
    scripts, and pricing strategy are Company property; the Representative shall not
    divert clients or leads off-platform or use Company data outside authorized
    program activities.
12. **Electronic acceptance.** This Agreement is accepted electronically through the
    Representative's WeShare dashboard; the Company records the agreement version,
    acceptance timestamp, account identity, and IP address as the signature record.

This constitutes the entire Agreement, together with the documents incorporated by
reference above. This Agreement shall be binding upon the parties and their
successors and assigns.

**Accepted electronically** — version v2-2026-07-09, recorded per §12.
