## Compliance Footnotes (govern every asset in this pack)

- **FTC — income claims and endorsements.** Publish only documented comp-plan facts. Any hypothetical earnings math must be explicitly labeled illustrative and immediately followed by the standard disclaimer line used in the listings above; every job post ends with that line regardless. Testimonials or partner endorsements used in recruiting must reflect real, typical experience and disclose the material connection (FTC Endorsement Guides, 16 CFR Part 255).
- **TCPA — Sales Partners' outbound calling.** Outbound calls are placed only between 8:00 a.m. and 9:00 p.m. in the call recipient's local time zone. Scrub against the National Do Not Call Registry and OrenGen's internal DNC list before dialing, and honor every do-not-call request immediately and permanently.
- **Recruiting SMS.** Sent only to applicants and Partners who provided their number; every template carries "Reply STOP to opt out," and STOP is honored immediately. No misleading urgency, no third-party link shorteners — branded weshare.orengen.io links only.
- **1099 status.** Every activity threshold in this pack is a software-access term tied to the company-sponsored seat, line, and lead feed — never a work schedule, dial quota, or job duty. Sales Partners control their own hours, methods, and volume at all times.
- **Rescission.** The 72-Hour Lock mirrors the Texas right of rescission (Tex. Bus. & Com. Code ch. 601): if a client rescinds inside the statutory window, the related commission voids before payout; from hour 73 the commission is locked and payable on the Friday run.
