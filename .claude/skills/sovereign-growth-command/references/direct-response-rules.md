# Direct-Response Rules

The conversion physics every page, email, and script obeys.

## Page rules

- One page, one job, one primary CTA. Clear headline → outcome subheadline → CTA above the fold.
- Body spine: **pain → mechanism → proof → offer → CTA.** The mechanism is the named "how" that makes the promise believable.
- Social proof lives adjacent to every form and CTA, not quarantined in a carousel at the bottom.
- FAQ exists to execute objections identified in the profile, in the buyer's own words.
- CTA buttons visually dominant (accent color per Brand Guard), verb-first, outcome-flavored ("Get My Build Plan" beats "Submit").
- Mobile-first, low page weight, fast render. A funnel that loads slow is a funnel that leaks — set a speed budget and hold it.
- Short forms for low-ticket; application depth for high-ticket (friction as a qualifier, not an accident).
- Accessibility floor: real contrast (Brand Guard tokens pass), labeled inputs, keyboard-navigable.
- Compliance blocks (consent checkboxes, disclaimers) styled as part of the design, never hidden in mouse-print.

## Copy rules

- Specificity beats superlatives: numbers, timeframes, named mechanisms. (Forbidden-phrase lint already bans the superlative slop.)
- Every claim traceable to proof on file. Every guarantee honorable. Every urgency real.
- Write to one reader (the ICP's best buyer), in Trusted Counselor voice, Boardroom-to-Block register.

## Pricing display rules

Covered in modes/03-offer.md — no fake discounts, charm pricing only when configured, "Most Popular" only at 3+ tiers, annual-vs-monthly comparison only when configured, setup vs. recurring always separated.

## §Money Math (the formulas every offer and optimize pass runs)

```
AOV        = core price
             + (bump price × bump take rate)
             + (upsell price × upsell take rate)
             + (downsell price × downsell take rate × decline rate of upsell)
Gross margin (GM%) = (AOV − fulfillment cost per order) / AOV
LTV        = AOV × purchases per year × retention years   (service/recurring: MRR × gross margin × avg months)
CAC ceiling = LTV / 3          (configurable; 3:1 is the default floor)
Breakeven ROAS = 1 / GM%
Payback    = CAC / monthly gross profit per customer      (cash-engine target: ≤30–60 days)
```

Planning defaults until actuals exist: bump 20–35%, upsell 10–25%, downsell save 15–30%. /og-optimize replaces defaults with reality and the doc updates — assumptions are placeholders, not beliefs.

**The sanity check:** if LTV:CAC can't plausibly clear 3:1 with honest inputs, the offer, the price, or the channel is wrong. Fix the offer; never fix the spreadsheet.
