# Lead Scoring Rubric

Ten dimensions, 100 points, grades A–F. Same rubric serves /og-leads (tactical) and /og-opportunities (strategic, with re-weighting noted below). Weights are starting values — the Evolution rule updates them with real outcomes.

## Dimensions & starting weights

| Dimension | Weight | What earns points |
|---|---|---|
| ICP fit | 20 | Matches profile.icp on vertical, size, geography, buyer role |
| Pain urgency | 15 | Evidence the pain is active now (hiring for it, complaining publicly, deadline-driven) |
| Budget probability | 15 | Revenue signals, current spend on alternatives, funding/contract awards |
| Authority probability | 10 | Contact is/reaches the economic buyer |
| Offer fit | 10 | The ladder has a rung purpose-built for this pain |
| Timing | 10 | Trigger event present (new role, new location, lost vendor, new regulation, contract expiry) |
| Need clarity | 5 | They can articulate the problem (or it's visibly measurable) |
| Channel accessibility | 5 | A compliant path to reach them exists |
| Compliance safety | 5 | Clean consent path; no regulated-contact landmines — **hard gate below** |
| Revenue potential | 5 | Deal size × expansion likelihood |

Strategic accounts (/og-opportunities): shift +5 from `need clarity`/`channel accessibility` into `revenue potential` and add competitive-gap judgment to the research brief.

## Grades

| Grade | Score | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **A** | ≥85 | Immediate priority — personalized outreach draft → approval queue |
| **B** | 70–84 | Qualified nurture — sequence assignment |
| **C** | 50–69 | Low priority — quarterly re-score |
| **D** | 30–49 | Disqualified — reason logged |
| **F** | <30 or gate-fail | Do not contact — registry entry |

**Hard gates (override any score):** compliance-safety fail → F. Explicit opt-out → F. Profile's "worst buyer" match → D minimum. Never argue with a gate.

## Evolution rule

Every 25 closed outcomes (won + lost), compare dimension scores of winners vs. losers. Move weight (max ±5 per cycle, total always = 100) toward dimensions that separated winners from losers; log the change, the evidence, and the date in `winners-library.md`; re-grade the active pool. Scoring that never learns is decoration.
