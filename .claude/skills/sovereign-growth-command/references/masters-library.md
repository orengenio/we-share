# Masters Library

The modeled-masters registry: every "top of the world" doctrine source SOVEREIGN draws from, what each contributes, and where it lives in the system. **Rule of the library: study the masters, ship the voiceprint.** We model their *patterns and disciplines* — we never imitate their voice (Edge Engine Law: sound like no one else, including the masters), never fabricate their endorsement, and verify their current work per `references/source-verification.md` before citing specifics.

## Extension protocol (for the next "top 3 in the world" batch)

When a new masters batch arrives: 1) verify the people/systems and their current work (source-verification), 2) extract the *transferable discipline* — the operating pattern, not the personality, 3) register the row here with its core lesson, 4) route the discipline into an existing mode or commission a new one, 5) never quote or attribute without a verified source, never imply endorsement. The library compounds like everything else here — masters in, doctrine distilled, voiceprint out.
