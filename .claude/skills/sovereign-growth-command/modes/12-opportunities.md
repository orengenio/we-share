# Mode 12 — /og-opportunities

The Career-Ops pattern, converted from job-hunting to deal-hunting: scan → research → score → grade → next best action. Never use job-search language unless the business model is actually recruiting/career services.

## Opportunity classes

local businesses (AI agency / web design / automation prospects) · consulting prospects · **government opportunities** (federal + SLED) · partnership opportunities · referral-partner candidates · sales-agent candidates · white-label software clients · strategic accounts.

## Government lane (Mode O advantage)

When the Tango govcon MCP is connected: run live opportunity + forecast scans filtered to profile NAICS/PSC codes and set-asides the entity actually holds. Cross-check every set-aside claim against Brand Guard's certification list — **never** chase or claim set-asides not on file (no HUB, no VetHUB). Score govcon opportunities on: agency fit, incumbent strength, set-aside eligibility, response runway, contract vehicle access, and realistic p-win — a small % of a right-sized pursuit beats a lottery-ticket mega-bid.

## Opportunity board (one row per target)

```
target | class | value estimate | urgency | decision maker | pain evidence
recommended offer (ladder rung) | recommended funnel | recommended outreach channel
grade A–F | next best action | follow-up date | GHL pipeline stage
```

Grading uses the same 10-dimension rubric as leads (`references/lead-scoring-rubric.md`) with `revenue potential` and `competitive gap` weighted up for strategic accounts. Compliance safety remains a hard gate.

## Rules

- Every A-grade opportunity gets a one-paragraph research brief with at least two verifiable specifics before any outreach drafts.
- **Handoffs:** A-grade RFPs/solicitations route to /og-capture for the full capture→write→negotiate loop; grant-shaped opportunities route to /og-grants for fit scoring and narrative build.
- Outreach drafts go to the approval queue like everything else.
- Board syncs to GHL as an `Opportunities` pipeline via /og-ghl-sync; grade changes move stages automatically once wired.

## Produce (`artifacts/<slug>/opportunities/`)

`opportunity-board.md`, `research-briefs/`, approval-queue entries, and (govcon) `pursuit-calendar.md` with response deadlines.
