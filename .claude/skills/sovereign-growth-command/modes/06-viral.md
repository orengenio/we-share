# Mode 06 — /og-viral

The content war machine (GoViralBro pattern, SOVEREIGN discipline). Requires: locked offer, profile category H. Read `winners-library.md` FIRST — repeat what won before inventing. Rules and taxonomy: `references/viral-content-rules.md`.

## Pipeline

```
DISCOVER → ANGLE → HOOK → SCRIPT → CREATIVE → DISTRIBUTE → ANALYZE → EVOLVE
```

- **DISCOVER** — competitor + aspirational-creator intake, topic mining (YouTube transcript ingestion, platform research via permitted APIs, manual URL paste fallback). Score topics: audience pain intensity × offer relevance × proof available.
- **ANGLE** — 10 angles per campaign, each tied to a specific ICP pain from the profile.
- **HOOK** — 30 hooks per campaign across the 10-category taxonomy (contrarian, pain, proof, mistake, status/authority, speed/result, risk-reversal, curiosity, industry secret, cost-of-inaction).
- **SCRIPT/ASSET** — the campaign quota below.
- **DISTRIBUTE** — content calendar + repurposing matrix. Posting is staged to the approval queue, never auto-published.
- **ANALYZE/EVOLVE** — import performance in /og-optimize; winners and losers written to `winners-library.md`.

## Campaign quota (per campaign, minimum)

30 hooks · 10 angles · 10 short-form video scripts · 5 LinkedIn posts · 5 X threads · 5 IG captions · 5 TikTok/Reels scripts · 3 YouTube titles + 3 descriptions · 3 lead-magnet concepts (PDF-ready outlines) · 3 ad scripts · 1 content calendar · 1 repurposing matrix.

## The mapping law

Every single asset carries a footer block:

```
buyer: <ICP segment> | pain: <specific pain> | offer: <ladder rung>
CTA: <exact ask> | destination: <funnel page> | GHL tag: <tag> | pipeline outcome: <stage>
```

Content that can't state its destination doesn't ship. Vanity content is a defect.

## Voice and brand

Boardroom-to-Block per Brand Guard: intelligent but plain-language, confident humor allowed, hype-slop forbidden. Founder-led content uses **"Mandel"** — never the legal surname. Every asset clears the Edge Engine (`references/edge-engine.md`, score ≥7) before its Brand Guard pass — hooks that could be anyone's don't ship. Pull hook raw material from persona voice-of-customer banks when they exist. Forbidden-phrase lint runs on every asset. Multilingual variants route through Buy-Lingual™ localization when configured. Audience-growth content (follow/subscribe-optimized) is /og-audience's lane — this mode stays conversion-first.

## Produce (`artifacts/<slug>/viral/`)

`campaign-<name>/hooks.md`, `angles.md`, `scripts/`, `posts/`, `lead-magnets/`, `ad-scripts.md`, `calendar.md`, `repurposing-matrix.md` — plus approval-queue entries for anything scheduled.
