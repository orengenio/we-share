# WeShare by OrenGen — Affiliate Handbook
**Rules of the Program · Standard Operating Procedures · v1.0 (June 2026)**

Welcome to WeShare. You share OrenGen's website offer with businesses that need it. When they become paying clients, you get paid — upfront and every month they stay. This handbook is the complete rulebook. Read it once; it protects your money.

---

## 1 · How You Earn

Every client pays OrenGen **$997 setup + $247/month maintenance**. Your cut depends on your rank:

| Rank | Unlock (lifetime sales) | Per Sale | Monthly Residual | Army Override |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ⚡ Catalyst | Join free | $99.70 (10%) | $12.35/mo × 12 mo | — |
| 🔨 Builder | 3 | $149.55 (15%) | $12.35/mo × 24 mo | 5% of recruits' sales, 12 mo |
| 📐 Architect | 10 | $199.40 (20%) | $18.53/mo **for life** | 5%, 24 mo |
| 👑 Sovereign | 25 | $249.25 (25%) | $24.70/mo **for life** | 5% **for life** + 2.5% residual override |

**The rules that make WeShare different:**
- **You can never lose your rank.** Promotions are permanent and fire instantly on the qualifying sale.
- **Fast Start Bonus:** +$50 if your first sale lands within 14 days of joining.
- **Milestone bonuses:** $100 at Builder · $500 at Architect · $1,500 at Sovereign — one-time cash, automatic.
- **Residuals are payment-driven.** You earn each month the client actually pays. Client churns or refunds → that residual stops. Nothing you did wrong; that's how recurring income works.
- Rates are locked per deal at the moment it closes. A later rank-up applies to your *next* deals, not retroactively.

## 2 · Getting Started (Onboarding SOP)

1. **Apply** at weshare.orengen.io — free, 2 minutes, no purchase ever required.
2. **Approval** — most applications clear within 1 business day.
3. **Connect payouts** — complete Stripe onboarding from your dashboard (this is how we pay you and issue your 1099). No Stripe setup = no payouts released.
4. **Grab your link** — your default link is live immediately. Create unlimited campaign links from the dashboard.
5. **Share it** using the Ad Copy Pack and creatives in your dashboard.
6. **Track everything** — clicks, leads, sales, rank progress, and pending money update in real time.

## 3 · Daily Operating Playbook

Your only job: put your link in front of business owners who need a website (new businesses, outdated sites, no mobile presence, no booking/payments online).

**Proven plays:** local business Facebook groups (where rules allow), your own social content, DMs to businesses with weak websites, email to your network, business networking events with your QR code, content/SEO ("best website service for small business" style posts).

**A working rhythm that hits Builder in week one:** 10 meaningful shares or conversations per day. Volume → leads → our partners close them → you get paid.

You **never sell, quote, or invoice**. Send traffic; OrenGen's sales partners and checkout do the rest. If a prospect asks questions you can't answer, your link IS the answer.

## 4 · Attribution Rules (How Credit Is Assigned)

- Your link tags the visitor for **90 days**.
- When they submit a form, the lead is **locked to you permanently** — later clicks on anyone else's link can't take it.
- Before lock, last click wins.
- **What never counts:** your own purchases or accounts (self-referrals), leads already in OrenGen's house database, fake/incentivized form fills, and traffic from prohibited tactics in §6.

## 5 · Getting Paid

- **Schedule:** weekly, every Friday.
- **Minimum:** $25 — smaller balances roll forward automatically.
- **First payout** is held 14 days for standard verification. Every payout after is automatic.
- **Clawbacks:** if a client refunds within 30 days, the matching commission reverses as a negative ledger entry against your next payout. Your statement shows every entry to the penny.
- **Taxes:** you're an independent contractor. Stripe issues your 1099 automatically at year end.
- Every commission traces to a logged system event. If you ever dispute a number, we audit the ledger line by line (§9).

## 6 · Program Rules (The Law)

**Required:**
1. **Disclose.** Any post, video, or message promoting WeShare must include a clear affiliate disclosure (e.g., "#ad" or "I earn commissions from OrenGen"). This is FTC law, not our preference.
2. **Income claims:** use only OrenGen-published figures, always with the results-vary disclaimer (included in the Ad Copy Pack). Never promise, guarantee, or imply guaranteed income.
3. One account per person. Accurate information at all times.

**Prohibited — these end accounts:**
- Spam of any kind (bulk unsolicited email/SMS/DMs violating platform rules or law)
- Bidding on "OrenGen," "WeShare," or misspellings in paid search; using our trademarks in domains or handles
- Cookie stuffing, forced clicks, iframes, link cloaking that hides the destination
- Self-referrals, fake leads, incentivized or paid-for form fills
- Misrepresenting the offer, pricing, or what OrenGen delivers
- Promoting on content involving anything illegal, hateful, or adult

## 7 · Army Builder Rules (Recruiting Affiliates)

- Unlocks at **Builder** — you sell 3 before you sponsor anyone. Earn first, then lead.
- You earn overrides **only when your recruits make real customer sales.** Recruiting itself pays $0 — always has, always will.
- **One level deep.** Your recruits' recruits are theirs, not yours.
- Joining is always free — never charge, collect, or accept anything from a recruit.
- Recruit with your link pointed at the WeShare signup page. They lock to you the same way leads do.

## 8 · Enforcement

- **Strike system:** minor violations (missing disclosure, unapproved claim) = written strike + 7 days to cure. **Three strikes = termination.**
- **Instant termination, no strikes:** fraud, fake leads, self-referrals, spam, trademark abuse, charging recruits.
- Termination for cause forfeits unpaid and future commissions. Termination without cause (program changes) pays out everything earned.
- You may close your account anytime; earned commissions above the minimum pay out on the next cycle.

## 9 · Disputes & Support

File any payout or attribution dispute from your dashboard within **30 days** of the statement date. We trace the ledger and rule within **5 business days**, in writing. Support: partners@orengen.io.
